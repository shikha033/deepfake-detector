Understanding Deepfake Detection

Deepfakes use artificial intelligence to manipulate videos and images, making them look real. This technology poses a serious threat to digital security by spreading misinformation.

How Our AI Detects Deepfakes

  • Analyzes facial inconsistencies and lighting mismatches.
  • Uses AI models trained on real vs. fake data.
  • Provides confidence scores for authenticity.
